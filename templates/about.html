About us


Hms General Hospital & Speciality Surgical Centre


Hms General Hospital & Speciality Surgical Centre is located just 500 meters away from the State Highhway near Shornur road, Thrissur, Kerala. We trace our beginning to 2001. It was the dawn of the new millennium and everyone was waiting for a change for the betterment of the humanity. It began as a vision of Dr. V. K Abdul Azeez and a team of Doctors to provide quality and affordable healthcare service to the common man. Hms has carved its own niche in the healthcare sector of Kerala by offering affordable and superior medical care to its patients. The hospital lives up to its motto, "Caring with Compassion"
